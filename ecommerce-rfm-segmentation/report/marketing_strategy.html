Executive Summary

The Core Insight

Our analysis reveals that ~25% of customers — Champions and Loyal Customers — generate approximately 55–65% of total revenue. Meanwhile, high-value segments like Cannot Lose Them and At Risk represent significant churn risk if left untargeted. A one-size-fits-all marketing strategy is leaving substantial revenue on the table. This report prescribes specific, segment-level marketing strategies to maximise ROI across the full customer base.
